About Me

Hello! I'm Anna!

I'm a graphic designer in Erie, PA. I graduated from Mercyhurst University in 2015 with a BA in Graphic Design and a minor in Digital Media. I've spent the past 4 years honing my skills at Altman-Hall Associates (2015-2016) and VertMarkets (2016-current).

I have a passion for design and have always had an eye for what makes a piece stand out. I'm a lifelong learner and I'm always seeking out new and better ways to do my job. My many skills and software knowledge include InDesign, Photoshop, & Illustrator HTML/CSS, Branding & Identity Development, Advertising, and Web Design.

In my free time I enjoy my retro video game collection, knitting, and binge-watching Netflix.
