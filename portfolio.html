Fairview Tigers Athletic Logo

This logo was created for the Fairview School District. It is an athletic logo to be used with all age levels (grades K-12). The challenge was to create a distinct tiger that isn't too frightening for younger kids, but wasn't too childish for the high school students.

Client: Fairview School District Date: April 2015 Service: Logo Design

WPUPC Collateral

This collection utilizes individual fingerprints to form semi-abstract brains to symbolize the individuality of everyone's mind. This design includes a program cover, t-shirt, tote bag, buttons, directional and promotional posters.

Client: Mercyhurst University Psychology Department Date: September 2014 Service: Print & Collateral Design

Nutrition By Blair Logos

Blair is a Nutritional Therapy Practitioner who works with clients to identify their body's nutritional imbalances and create a plan to correct these imbalances. My team worked with Blair to discover the core purpose and feel of her company. We then developed logos to fit the "feel" and created a brand. Above are two logos presented to Blair.

Client: Nutrition by Blair Date: April 2015 Service: Logo Design

Hydrastar Brake Line Illustration

This is a 3D model to illustrate the wiring of the brake lines on a Hydrastar brake actuator. The 3D model was built from assets and the brake line was hand drawn in Illustrator. This model was used in brochures, websites, and trade show displays.

Client: Hydrastar Date: February 2016 Service: Digital Illustration

The Lost Levels Branding & Website

This branding exercise was created for the course Corporate Branding and Identity during my time at Mercyhurst University. This project involved creating a fictional company, and creating the branding and guidelines surrounding the brand. The company I created was The Lost Levels, a used video game store. The four icons in the logo each identify a different aspect in the store. This branding received a 2016 Silver Erie Addy Award.

View the Brand Standards Manual.

Client: Hydrastar Date: February 2016 Service: Digital Illustration
