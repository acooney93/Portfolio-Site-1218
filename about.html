Hello! I'm Anna!

I'm a graphic designer in Erie, PA. I graduated from Mercyhurst University in 2015 with a BA in Graphic Design and a minor in Digital Media. I've spent the past 4 years honing my skills at Altman-Hall Associates (2015-2016) and VertMarkets (2016-current).

I have a passion for design and have always had an eye for what makes a piece stand out. I'm a lifelong learner and I'm always seeking out new and better ways to do my job. My many skills and software knowledge include InDesign, Photoshop, & Illustrator HTML/CSS, Branding & Identity Development, Advertising, and Web Design and Front End Web Development.

Always trying to round out my skills and place myself ahead, I am currently learning about animation with After Effects, CSS Grid for web development, and Adobe XD for web & app design. You can see my class projects over at SkillShare.

In my free time I enjoy my retro video game collection, knitting, and binge-watching Netflix.

What is a graphic designer?

Designers = Problem Solvers

At their core, all designers are problem solvers. The problems graphic designers solve are those like "we need to get the word out about our new project." What is the best way to achieve this objective? We do this by listening to the client, absorbing what they have to say, and developing that into the best solution to this problem. The fact that we also happen to make it look nice at the same time, is a very nice bonus.
